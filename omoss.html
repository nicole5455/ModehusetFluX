Our story

Flux is the result of Vera's passion for fashion and clothing. After traveling the world and designing for various companies, she felt it was time to create something entirely her own. Since her childhood, Vera has had a deep love for fashion and clothing, always enjoying blending different aesthetic styles into something unique. In 1995, together with her partner Karl and Virgil, the founded Modehuset Flux.

Our Style: Timeless Elegance

Our design philosophy is an exciting fusion of New York's vibrant creativity and Stockholm's sophisticated elegance. At Modehuset Flux, we strongly believe in timeless style that transcends trends and seasons. Our clothes are designed to be as beautiful today as they will be tomorrow. Timeless elegance is at the core of our brand, and we strive to create pieces that can be worn confidently for any occasion.

Our Commitment to the Environment

At Modehuset Flux, we care about our planet and are committed to reducing our environmental impact. We meticulously select the materials and fabrics used in our collections, always with sustainability in mind. By choosing high-quality and eco-friendly materials, we aim to create clothes that are not only beautiful but also responsible. Our commitment to the environment is part of our identity, and we continually work to decrease our carbon footprint and support sustainable production methods.
